FAQ's

Do you accept medical insurance for services?

We value the privacy of everyone that walks through our doors. This includes any diagnoses or treatment plans being offered. At Mindful Connections, LLC, your confidentiality is of utmost importance. We are currently working with several insurance providers in the area to become credentialed, and are currently credentialed with UnitedHealth Care, Security Health, Quartz, Medica and Medicaid. Please be patient with us as this is new to Mindful Connections. Inquire about plans with your therapist.


If you would like to submit your invoice to your insurance company for reimbursement, Mindful Connections, LLC can provide the necessary information to do so with a signed release of information.

How long will therapy last?

This is a difficult question to answer because it really depends on everyone's individual needs. Typically, therapy sessions last 50 minutes, but some forms of treatment, specifically EMDR, can last longer. At Mindful Connections, LLC, a treatment plan is tailored to fit your specific needs and concerns.

What does therapy look like?

Also different for everyone, therapy traditionally starts with a free 30 minute telehealth appointment with your therapist to ensure it's a good fit. This will be followed by an intake appointment where your therapist will ask you for your medical and social history. It is during this appointment that therapy goals are discussed and a potential treatment plan is created. When receiving mental health treatment we understand there will be times you may feel uncomfortable or unsure. We invite you to share these experiences with us so that we may tailor your experience to meet your individual needs.
